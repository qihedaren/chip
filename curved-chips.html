Why are Canned Potato Chips Curved?

The distinctive curve of potato chips isn't just for style - it's actually a result of the manufacturing process! Here's why:

When potatoes are sliced and fried, the moisture difference between the top and bottom surfaces causes the slice to curl naturally.

The science behind it:

  • The top surface loses moisture faster than the bottom
  • This creates tension in the potato slice
  • The tension causes the slice to curve upward
  • The curve is then "locked in" during the frying process
